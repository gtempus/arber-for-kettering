My Priorities for Kettering's Future

As a member of Kettering City Council, I will bring a collaborative voice and a commitment to these key priorities:

Priority 1: Protecting & Enhancing Kettering's Quality of Life

  • Focus: Safeguarding our unique parks, recreation facilities, and arts and culture programs. Our parks and recreation programs are a core part of what makes Kettering special, and I will be a strong advocate for their continued excellence.

  • Action: I will work to ensure our Police and Fire Departments are fully supported to keep Kettering safe and will champion investments in our critical infrastructure to create a strong, resilient community.

Priority 2: Fostering Responsible Economic Development & Thoughtful Growth

  • Focus: Continuing to provide amazing amenities requires us to attract new businesses to Kettering. I will focus on strategic growth that respects our landlocked nature and enhances our community, not burdens it.

  • Action: I will work to strategically utilize existing spaces and empty buildings to attract new businesses and jobs, generating revenue to support city services without over-relying on residents.

Priority 3: Ensuring Informed & Transparent Decisions

  • Focus: Bringing an experienced voice to the council to make decisions that reflect residents' best interests.

  • Action: I will work to promote open dialogue, collaboration, and accountability in city governance. My goal is to be a council member who is accessible and a true representative of our residents.
