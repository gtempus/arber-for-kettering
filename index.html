My Message to You

I am committed to being a council member who listens and represents the voices of our community.

Why I am Running

My experience in helping create the updated City of Kettering strategic plan showed me the importance of being at the decision-making table, and my long-term involvement with Kettering's Parks Board and Arts Council has given me a deep appreciation for the services that make our city so vibrant.

A Lifelong Commitment to Kettering

  • Born and raised in the surrounding area
  • Homeowner in Kettering

My Priorities for Kettering

  • Foster opportunities for sustainable economic growth
  • Ensure that the city infrastructure supports a vibrant future
