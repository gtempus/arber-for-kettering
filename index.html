Our Platform

Economic Opportunity

Plan to create jobs and support small businesses through targeted incentives and streamlined permitting.

Neighborhood Safety

Sustainable Infrastructure

Modernize roads and utilities with green drainage solutions and smart technology.

Expand parks and recreation programs for all ages, including senior centers and youth activities.

Implement zero-based budgeting to ensure every tax dollar is spent wisely.

Transparent Government

Live-stream all council meetings and create an online budget transparency portal.
